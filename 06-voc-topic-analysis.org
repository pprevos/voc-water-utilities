The purpose of topic modelling is to understand the structure of a text corpus. We can, for example, analyse a collection of social media messages about tap water and let the model decide what topics they discuss, such as taste, price and pressure.

In topic modelling, each document is represented as a predetermined number of topics. Each topic is a distribution over words.
